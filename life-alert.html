Life Alert

PERSONAL EMERGENCY RESPONDER MOBILE APP

Role: UX Designer

Overview

For a school project, we focused on extending the company Life Alert's product to appeal to a younger audience made up of avid smart phone users who are seeking a discreet answer for personal protection on the go.

Problem

Life Alert has a problem with its antiquated brand image that relies on a slogan from the 1980's and whose current product serves an older demographic dependent on landline phones.

Solution

A mobile app that lets users connect to a trained dispatcher when they are feeling unsafe and lets them connect to 911 during an emergency. Users can share their location with a trusted circle of emergency contacts.

The Team

I worked on a team with two other UX designers during a two-week sprint at General Assembly.

My Role

User researcher and UX designer.
